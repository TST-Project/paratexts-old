Colophons

In the TST Project, we define a colophon as whatever text that comes after the end of the work (root-text or commentary), that is, a colophon may include a text completion statement, blessings, the colophon proper (that we call and tag copy statement), a phalaśruti (statement of benefits to the reader, listener; which we call and tag commenting note), etc.
